Welcome

The Art Stable is your inner city sanctuary in the heart of the creative Cuba quarter in Wellington, the ‘coolest little capital’ in the world. Located in a building that started life in the 1880s as a stable, this boutique apartment combines quality, character and culture to create distincitve accommodation - a peaceful, art-filled retreat in the inner city.

For the past 12 years, the Art Stable was situated above Bartley & Company Art, one of the city’s leading contemporary art galleries. Recently the gallery moved to a bigger space nearby, across the park at 22 Garrett Street, but the apartment remains distinctively furnished with top quality art.

Totally self-contained, the Art Stable features one bedroom with a super king or two single beds in the bedroom and a sofa bed in the separate open-plan living room. Double glazing and luxurious Egyptian cotton sheets and quality linen should ensure a good night’s sleep. However if you open the windows sounds does come in and, as we are in the inner city, noise at night is inevitable. We do provide earplugs for those who need them.

A full kitchen, with light breakfast provisions, is available. If you have special dietary requirements, for example you are gluten free, please let us know and we will do our best to meet your needs. With so many cafes right on the doorstep, you may just want to eat out. The TV, on the off-chance you want a quiet evening at home, has Chromecast installed. If you subscribe to Netflix, Neon, Spark Sport or any streaming service you can cast it onto the Art Stable TV.

The owner Alison lives in the adjacent apartment. A born and bred Wellingtonian, she can provide advice on all the best places to see and eat as well as things to do. Just 500 metres from the city’s award winning waterfront, the Art Stable offers a warm retreat and easy walking access to everything the city has to offer – from inner city shopping and entertainment to harbour, beach and bush.

Most of the year, we are happy to have one night stays but cannot accept these during holiday periods such as Christmas and Easter and during special events in Wellington.

We welcome business travellers. Our prices include 15% goods and services tax which New Zealand business travellers will be able claim back.

Or via BookIt for credit card payments & full payment is required.

The Art Stable's check in is 3pm, check out 11am. We are happy to accommodate your requirements so if you need to check in or out outside of these times, please contact us.

Cancellation Policy

  • Our policy is driven by the likelihood of us being able to rebook after your cancellation.
  • Cancelling up to two weeks before your scheduled reservation date your deposit is fully refundable.
  • Within two weeks of the booking commencing, half of the deposit will be refunded.
  • Within seven days of your booking, your deposit will not be refunded.
